My Sightings

You can record your sightings and see the sightings of other visitors at: iNaturalist Parishville Park

Download the iNaturalist app, available in Google Play (Android) or App Store (iPhone), and begin adding your sightings via the app or from the iNaturalist website (www.iNaturalist.org). You simply need to create an account, and you can add your sightings via your phone or later from your computer. iNaturalist will help you identify what you are seeing.

For a current list of species seen at Parishville Park go to: Parishville Park iNaturalist

Welcome to the Parishville Nature Trail! This trail is designed to highlight some of the special features of the West Branch of the St. Regis River, as well as the surrounding woodland.
